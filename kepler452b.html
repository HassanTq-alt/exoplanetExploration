About Kepler-452B

Kepler-452B is a super-Earth-sized exoplanet orbiting a G-type star. It is located in the habitable zone of its star, where liquid water could exist.

Key Facts

  • Radius: 1.6 times Earth's radius
  • Orbital Period: 384.8 days
  • Distance from Earth: 1,402 light-years
  • Host Star: Kepler-452
