About TRAPPIST-1f

TRAPPIST-1f is one of seven planets orbiting the ultra-cool dwarf star TRAPPIST-1. It's a rocky planet with the potential to be habitable.

Key Facts

  • TRAPPIST-1f is a potentially habitable exoplanet located in the TRAPPIST-1 system, about 40 light-years away. It's a rocky planet, slightly smaller than Earth, and lies within the star's habitable zone, where conditions might allow liquid water to exist. The planet's cool temperature suggests it could support a thick atmosphere, making it a candidate for further study in the search for life beyond our solar system.
  • Radius: 1.045 times Earth's radius
  • Orbital Period: 9.2 days
  • Distance from Earth: 39.5 light-years
  • Host Star: TRAPPIST-1
