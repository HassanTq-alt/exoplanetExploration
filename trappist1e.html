About TRAPPIST-1e

TRAPPIST-1e lies within the habitable zone of its host star. Scientists believe it could have liquid water on its surface.

Key Facts

  • Radius: 0.92 times Earth's radius
  • Orbital Period: 6.1 days
  • Distance from Earth: 39.5 light-years
  • Host Star: TRAPPIST-1
